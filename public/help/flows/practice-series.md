# Practice Series Help

Explore yoga series - collections of related poses organized by theme or focus area.

## What is a Series?

A series is a curated collection of yoga poses grouped by:
- **Body part focus** (hip openers, backbends, etc.)
- **Therapeutic goal** (stress relief, strength building)
- **Tradition** (Ashtanga, Iyengar, etc.)

## How to Use

1. **Browse series** - Scroll through available collections
2. **Select a series** - Click to see all poses in the series
3. **Practice** - Follow the poses in any order
4. **Create your own** - Build custom series for your practice

## Tips

- Series can be practiced in any order
- Combine series for longer practice sessions
- Bookmark favorites for quick access
