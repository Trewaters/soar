# Practice Flow Help

Explore yoga flows - collections of related poses organized by theme or focus area.

## What is a Flow?

A flow is a curated collection of yoga poses grouped by:

- **Body part focus** (hip openers, backbends, etc.)
- **Therapeutic goal** (stress relief, strength building)
- **Tradition** (Ashtanga, Iyengar, etc.)

## How to Use

1. **Browse flows** - Scroll through available collections
2. **Select a flow** - Click to see all poses in the flow
3. **Practice** - Follow the poses in any order
4. **Create your own** - Build custom flows for your practice

## Tips

- Flow can be practiced in any order
- Combine flows for longer practice sessions
- Bookmark favorites for quick access
