# Practice Sequences Help

Practice complete yoga sequences - structured flows combining multiple series into a complete practice session.

## What is a Sequence?

A sequence is an ordered collection of yoga series that creates a complete practice journey:

- **Structured flow** - Series arranged in a specific order
- **Complete practice** - Beginning, middle, and end progression
- **Guided experience** - Move through each series step by step

## How to Use

1. **Search for a sequence** - Use the search bar to find sequences by name
2. **Navigate through series** - Use arrow buttons or pagination circles to move between series
3. **View each series** - Click series names to see detailed pose lists
4. **Track your practice** - Mark sequences as completed to monitor progress
5. **Switch views** - Toggle between scroll (current) and list view using icons

## View Options

- **Scroll View** - Practice one series at a time with navigation controls
- **List View** - See all series in the sequence at once

## Tips

- Follow sequences in order for best results
- Practice regularly and track your progress
- Click on individual poses to view detailed instructions
- Use the activity tracker to build consistency
