# Create Asana Help

Add a new yoga pose to your personal collection.

## Required Information

- **English Name** - Primary name for the pose
- **Sanskrit Name** - Traditional Sanskrit name
- **Difficulty Level** - Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced

## Optional Information

- **Description** - Detailed explanation of the pose
- **Benefits** - Physical and mental benefits
- **Setup Cues** - Instructions for entering the pose
- **Alignment Cues** - Proper body alignment tips
- **Images** - Upload photos of the pose

## Tips

- Use proper Sanskrit spelling with diacritics if possible
- Include modifications for different skill levels
- Add personal notes about your practice
